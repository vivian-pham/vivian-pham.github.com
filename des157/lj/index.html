Overuse of Overlays

Learning about overlays and the Do's and Dont's helped me better understand its importance in a design aspect. An insighful method to determine whether or not lightboxes are appropriate are the 5 W's- who, what, when, where, and why, which I will go through and ask myself the next time I include a lightbox. I recently worked with a client who used overlays as a giant message board that required scrolling to read the entirety of its contents; I knew it wasn't a best practice but wasn't sure how to explain it but this article clarified a lot of the reasonsings behind it. Some important points to think about additionally are the audience, the action desired, when and where the overlay appears, if it interrupts anything, and why is it necessary to have it on a lightbox. Although these lightboxes can be a quick and easy way to add additional information, failure to put proper thought and consideration towards its purpose could actually lead to bad design and take away from your overall site. Lastly, I learned that it's very important to test your light boxes as well on different platforms to make sure that it is functioning properly.

January 20th

Best Practices for Form Design

In order to get the best results from your users, it is important to pay attention to a form's usability as an aspect of design itself. You should make the experience of filling out forms as clear, concise, and enjoyable as you can for your users to get the best results. This can be through your directions, placeholder texts, formatting instructions, security and privacy, and message responses and feedback. It's also important to note the accessibility level on not just your site but your form as well. Although it is important to get as much information you can from your users, you want to avoid cognitive load by reducing the number of fields and steps in your process. Filling out forms tend to be a tedious task but following the best practices for form design can help bring you more responses and traffic to reach your goal. After reading this article, I was able to dissect the different aspects of forms and the detail that should be placed on creating a better experience in all aspects.
